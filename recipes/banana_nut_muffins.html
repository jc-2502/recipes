Banana nut muffins

Description

Banana nut muffins

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup bran
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1-3 tbsp sugar
  • 1/2 cup chopped nuts
  • 4 ripe bananas
  • 1 egg
  • 1/3 cup oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Steps

  1. mix dry ingredients in large bowl: flour, bran, baking soda, baking powder, sugar, chopped nuts
  2. mix wet ingredients in another large bowl: bananas (mash in the bowl first), egg (beat first), oil, vanilla extract
  3. pour dry ingredient bowl contents into wet ingredient bowl and mix
  4. put 6 medium paper cups in muffin baking tray, fill each cup to top of paper cut, then add more on top
  5. bake at 375 f for 3 min then 325 f for 22 min
