Salmon cakes

Description

Salmon cakes

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 1 can salmon
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup plain greek yogurt or mayo
  • 1.5 tspn dijon mustard
  • 1 tspn lemon juice
  • 1 tspn onion powder
  • 1 tspn garlic powder
  • 1/4 tspn cayenne pepper
  • olive oil or other oil

Steps

  1. beat 1 egg
  2. drain and mash salmon, add to egg
  3. add rest of ingredients, mix
  4. make patties
  5. add oil to pan, turn heat to medium
  6. cook patties on one side for 3 min until golden brown
  7. flip and cook other side for 2 min
