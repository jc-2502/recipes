Lasagna

Description

Lasagna with meat sauce

Ingredients

  • 1 onion
  • garlic
  • ground meat
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 jar tomato puree/crushed tomatoes
  • herb mix
  • worcestershire sauce (optional)
  • 1 L vegetable stock
  • lasagna sheets
  • shredded cheese

Steps

  1. chop onion and garlic
  2. fry onion and garlic in pot
  3. add ground meat
  4. add tomatoes, herb mix, worcestershire sauce (optional)
  5. add vegetable stock
  6. simmer on low heat for 10-15 min
  7. place baking dish on metal tray
  8. add sauce in bottom of dish, then lasagna sheet, repeat layers, top layer should be sauce
  9. add shredded cheese on top, leaving ~ 1 cm room at top of dish
  10. bake at 200 degrees C for 50 min or until cheese is bubbling and starting to brown
